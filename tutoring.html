Pathway 01

Examination Preparation Pathway

Get focused 1:1 support for your GCSE, IB, A Level, or AP exams.

We know that achieving top grades isn’t just about learning content. We help you develop the techniques that drive performance under pressure:

  • Essay writing
  • Efficient memorisation
  • Time management
  • Revision planning

This pathway builds the resilience and lasting academic habits that elevate your academic confidence.

Pathway 02

Foundational Enrichment Pathway

Tackle undergraduate-level thinking about the subjects that inspire you with the guidance of an Oxford PhD researcher

90-minute sessions structured around primary sources, academic scholarship, and major intellectual debates in different fields. Tutors assign 25–40 pages of reading per week, drawn from books, articles, historical documents, and other materials selected for their accessibility and relevance.

Develop critical analysis and writing skills, culminating in a 3,000-word final project.

Pathway 03 · Fully customised

Bespoke Tutoring Pathway

Explore your interests beyond the curriculum with the 1:1 support of an Oxford PhD expert

Customised syllabi covering the content you want to learn at the pace that works for you. Explore a topic of your choice through academic reading, discussion, and written analysis. Work towards a final project (we recommend an extended essay or portfolio) that ties together your learning. Possible areas of inquiry include a deep dive into a historical period (e.g. Victorian England), a geographic region (e.g. the Middle East), or designing a scientific experiment (e.g. measuring the acidity of a liquid using red cabbage).
