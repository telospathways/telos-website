How we teach

Our method and tutor selection

We were students not too long ago. We know what teaching methods work.

01 — Our method
No dull textbooks, worksheets, or box-ticking.

We opt for 1:1 Oxford tutorial-style discussions grounded in:

  • Accessible explanations of complex ideas
  • Socratic questioning that pushes students to construct their own arguments
  • Primary sources and case studies: historical documents, news articles, research papers and science experiments
02 — Tutor selection
We hire the brightest Oxbridge and Ivy League graduate students.

Our tutors all possess:

  • Outstanding academic records in their undergraduate and postgraduate studies at Oxbridge or Ivy League universities
  • Teaching experience, often to Oxbridge undergraduate students
  • Emotional intelligence and patience
