Our perspective on the AI revolution

The problem
Over-relying on AI tools to write, read, and solve problems erodes human intelligence and deep thinking.
Students who outsource thinking to AI lose the ability to construct original and critical arguments.
Schools lack the frameworks to respond meaningfully to a technology evolving faster than policy.
Our approach
We help teachers integrate AI tools into lesson plans to elevate students’ critical thinking.
We design workshops that place human judgement at the centre of every task.
We work with schools to build lasting frameworks rather than reactive policies to tackle the AI age.
